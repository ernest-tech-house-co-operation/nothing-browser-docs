## Why Fund?

Nothing Browser, Nothing Private Browser, and Piggy are completely **free and open source** (MIT licensed). No paywalls. No subscriptions. No enterprise pricing.

But building and maintaining them takes time, effort, and resources.

Your funding helps:
- ✅ Keep the project alive
- ✅ Pay for development time
- ✅ Cover infrastructure costs (build servers, domain, R2 storage)
- ✅ Test on more platforms (macOS, Windows)
- ✅ Add new features faster

## FAQ

### Is Nothing Browser going to become paid?

**No.** The core will always be free and open source (MIT).

### Will there be a "pro" version?

**No.** No features are locked behind paywalls.

### Why not just rely on donations?

Because I'd rather build something useful and let people pay what they think it's worth. If you can't pay, that's fine — use it for free forever.

### Can my company sponsor development?

**Yes.** Email `ernesttechhouse@gmail.com` for corporate sponsorship arrangements.

### Do you accept crypto?

**Not yet.** Maybe in the future. Stick to PayPal for now.
