## Scraping Legality Note

**Web scraping of public data is not illegal in most jurisdictions.**

Courts have consistently ruled that:
- Publicly accessible data is not protected by CFAA
- Scraping is not trespassing
- Information wants to be free

However, some websites may ban your IP. Some may send cease and desist letters. That's between you and them.

**We don't provide legal advice. Consult a lawyer if you're unsure.**
